Amount of your vacation days are visible in your payslip on monthly basis.

To take a vacation

Email at: vacations.lt@visma.com
Email subject: Vacations or Atostogos
Email body: Prašau suteikti man kasmetines atostogas nuo YYYY-MM-DD iki YYYY-MM-DD imtinai

To take a parental leave (mamadienis/tėvadienis)

Email at: vacations.lt@visma.com
Email subject: Tevadienis or Tėvadienis
Email body: Prašau suteikti man mamadienį/tėvadienį YYYY-MM-DD

To take unpaid leave

Email at: vacations.lt@visma.com
Email subject: Neapmokamos atostogos
Email body: Prašau suteikti man neapmokamas atostogas nuo YYYY-MM-DD iki YYYY-MM-DD imtinai

To cancel your vacation request

In your "Sent" folder find the request which you want to cancel, then press "Reply" and type "cancel" in the email body.
Note: it only works for regular vacations!

What happens next?

Once your coach approves your vacations, you will receive an email from vacations.lt@visma.com with a subject line that says - "jusu atostogu prasymas PATVIRTINTAS." If you haven't received such an email - don't go on vacation. Ask your coach to process your request first.

Vacation compensation

You will receive a vacation payment at the end of month together with your salary payment. If you need money in advance, please, request your vacation in writing using a paper template that can be found and printed within Important Docs site on Simoona.
